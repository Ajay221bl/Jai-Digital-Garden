# mean ($\mu$)
sum of values divided by total number of values

- mean is a measure of [[Notes/central tendency\|central tendency]]

$$\bar{y} =\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n}y_{i}$$
where:
$\bar{y}$ refers to the [[sample mean\|sample mean]]
