# Probability distribution
A [[Notes/probability distributions\|probability distribution]] is a mathematical function that provides the [[Notes/probability\|probabilities]] of occurrence of different possible outcomes for an experiment


These distributions are fundamental to [[Notes/statistical analysis\|statistical analysis]], enabling us to model and understand the uncertainty inherent in various phenomena.


The probability distributions can be classified mainly into two types:
1. [[Notes/discrete probability distributions\|discrete probability distributions]]
2. [[Notes/continuous probability distributions\|continuous probability distributions]]
x

> understanding [[Notes/probability distributions\|probability distributions]] is imperative because they form the backbone of [[Notes/statistical inference\|statistical inference]] and decision-making process in various fields, including finance, engineering, biology, and social sciences.
