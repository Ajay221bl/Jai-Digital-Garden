# use cases of [[Notes/logistic regression\|logistic regression]]

- Banks predicting whether a loan applicant will default, using factors such as credit score, credit history, income, and (if allowed) demographic data
- Wireless carriers predicting the [[Notes/probability\|likelihood]] of customers canceling their service, based on usage patterns and satisfaction scores.
- Political scientists predicting election outcomes by analyzing the survey data
- Meteorologists predicting the [[Notes/probability\|Notes/probability]] of rain from a mix of satellite and radar data, atmospheric humidity, cloud cover, and other factors.
