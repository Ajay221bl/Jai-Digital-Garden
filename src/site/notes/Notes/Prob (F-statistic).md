# Prob (F-statistic)
Prob ([[Notes/F-statistic\|F-statistic]]) which is equal to $0.000107$, represents the [[Notes/p-value\|p-value]] associated with the [[Notes/F-statistic\|F-statistic]]. It indicates the [[Notes/probability\|Notes/probability]] of observing an [[Notes/F-statistic\|F-statistic]] as extreme, or even more extreme, than the one calculated from the data, under the assumption that the [[Notes/null hypothesis\|null hypothesis]] is true
