# rate parameter ($\lambda$)
gives the average rate of occurrence of an event.

In case of [[Notes/poisson distribution\|poisson distribution]], both [[Notes/mean\|mean]] and [[Notes/variance\|variance]] are equal to [[Notes/rate parameter\|rate parameter]]($\lambda$)
$$
\mu=\sigma^{2}=\lambda
$$
