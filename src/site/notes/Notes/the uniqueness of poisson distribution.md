# the uniqueness of poisson distribution
the [[Notes/poisson distribution\|poisson distribution]] is unique in sense that it seamlessly integrates empirical data with theoretical [[Notes/probability\|probabilities]] offering a practical tool for modeling real-world [[Notes/event\|events]] that occur independently over a fixed interval.
Unlike other [[Notes/probability distributions\|probability distributions]] that are more theoretical, the [[Notes/poisson distribution\|poisson distribution]] directly connects the observed data, making it invaluable for applications like traffic flow analysis, call center management, and biological processes.
