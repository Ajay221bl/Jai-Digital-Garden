# dependent events
two events are dependent if the outcome of one event influences the outcome of the other.


[[Notes/mutually exclusive events are not independent\|mutually exclusive events are not independent]], they are dependent

suppose we have two mutually exclusive events:
1. rolling a 3
2. rolling an even number

these are both [[Notes/mutually exclusive sets or events\|mutually exclusive]] events as its impossible to roll a 3 and even number at the same time using a single dice. But these are not [[Notes/independent events\|independent]] the occurrence of one event would influence the occurrence of second event. 
for example the die is rolled and we got 3. This reduced the probability of getting an even number on that same dice to zero for that particular instance.
