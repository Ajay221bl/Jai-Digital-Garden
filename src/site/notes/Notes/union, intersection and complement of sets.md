# union, intersection and complement of sets

$\text{S = \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6\}}$
$\text{A = \{1, 3, 5\}}$
$\text{B= \{1, 5, 6\}}$

$A \cap B = \{1, 5\}$
$A \cup B = \{1, 3, 5, 6 \}$

#### complementary
$A^{c} = \{2, 4, 6 \}$   (all the elements of S that are not in A)
$B^c = \{2, 3, 4 \}$   (all the elements of S that are not in B)
