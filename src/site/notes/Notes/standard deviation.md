# standard deviation ($\sigma$)
standard deviation is a measure that quantifies the amount of variation, or [[Notes/dispersion\|dispersion]] from the [[Notes/mean\|mean]]

- standard deviation measures the spread or [[Notes/dispersion\|dispersion]] of the [[Notes/probability distributions\|distribution]].


- The greater the standard deviation the more dispersed are the values from the [[Notes/mean\|mean]]. hence a very high standard deviation means flatter distributions and wider tails in case of [[Notes/normal distribution\|normal distribution]].


> a low [[Notes/standard deviation\|standard deviation]] indicates that most of the data is close to the [[Notes/mean\|mean]]
