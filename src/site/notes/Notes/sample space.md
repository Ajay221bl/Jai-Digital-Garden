# sample space
sample space is the collection of all outcomes or [[Notes/event\|events]] of an experiment

- here s is the sample space of throwing a dice.

the [[subset\|subset]] of a [[Notes/sample space\|sample space]] is called an [[Notes/event\|event]]

- when ===tossing two coins===: the [[Notes/sample space\|sample space]] would be:
	- { HH, TT, TH, HT }
	- the events for this sample space would  be:
		- atleast one heads: {ht, th, hh}
		- no heads: {tt}
		- no tails: {hh}
		- and so on 


the sample space's place is in the denominator
