# uncertain disciplines and [[Notes/probability\|Notes/probability]]
- disciplines like physics and biology exhibit uncertain characteristics:
	- quantum physics shows that the world beyond atomic level is quite uncertain
	- biology progresses through the accumulation of many random effects like mutations within [[Notes/uncertainty\|uncertain]] environment.
	- All the biological data that we are accumulating requires sifting using [[Notes/statistical analysis\|statistical analysis]]. Basically it is finding signal from the noise ([[Notes/uncertainty\|uncertainty]]).
	- markets are uncertain and it is important to create model of this uncertainty by using [[Notes/probability\|Notes/probability]]
