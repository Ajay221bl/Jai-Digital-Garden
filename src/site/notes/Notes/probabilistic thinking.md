# probabilistic thinking
it involves assigning likelihoods to different possible outcomes and then making decisions based on those probabilities.

Since we live in an uncertain world, we cannot be 100% sure that a certain event will occur in the future ([[Notes/prediction\|prediction]]), we have to rely on concept of [[Notes/probability\|Notes/probability]] to assign likelihood to as many situations that can possibly occur and then the situation that has the highest chance of happening is our best possible chance at predicting the future (though it can go totally wrong as it is relying on the historical data)
