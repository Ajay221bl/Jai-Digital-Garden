# [[Notes/probability\|Notes/probability]] of A intersection B

$S = \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6\}$           
$A=\{2, 4, 6 \}$
$C = \{1, 3, 5\}$

$P(A\cap C) = 0$            $\text{(probability of both A and C occurring together)}$

$\because \text{there is nothing common between A and C}$

the above is the [[Notes/probability\|Notes/probability]] of an [[Notes/empty set\|empty set]]

both A and C are [[Notes/mutually exclusive sets or events\|mutually exclusive sets or events]]
