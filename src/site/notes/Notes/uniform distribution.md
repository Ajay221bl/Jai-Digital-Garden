# uniform distribution

all outcomes within a specific range are equally likely. It is defined by a minimum and maximum value, a and b, respectively.

an example is a random generator that produces numbers between 0 and 1.



it could be classified further as [[Notes/continuous uniform distribution\|continuous uniform distribution]] or [[Notes/discrete uniform distribution\|discrete uniform distribution]] based on the type of the [[Notes/random variable\|random variable]] whether it is [[Notes/continuous random variables\|continuous]] or [[Notes/discrete random variables\|discrete]].
