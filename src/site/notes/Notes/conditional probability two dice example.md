# conditional probability two dice example



 
([[Notes/probability\|Notes/probability]] of event A intersecting event B) or in other words what is the probability of A and B both occurring together at the same time
$P(\,(D_1 = 2)\, \,\cap\,(D_1+ D_2 <=5))$           
$\huge{\frac{3}{36}}$               $\because \text{only three elements are intersecting between two events}$
 

> what if we already know that $D_1+D_2 <=5$ and we want to find out what is the probability that D_1 is equal to 2. This is called [[Notes/conditional probability\|conditional probability]]


$$P[(D_1=2)\,\,|\,\,(D_1+D_2<=5)] \,\,\,\,\large{=}\,\,\,\,\, \large\frac{P[D_1=2 \cap (D_1+D_2<=5]}{P[D_1+D_2<=5]}$$ 
$$\huge=\frac{\frac{3}{36}}{\frac{10}{36}}= \frac{3}{10}$$
