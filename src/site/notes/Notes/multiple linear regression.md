# multiple linear regression
the [[Notes/response variable\|dependent variable]] is regressed against let's say three [[Notes/explanatory variables\|predictors]]

$$
\gamma= \beta_{0}+ \beta_{1}x_{1}+\beta_{2}x_{2}+\beta_{3}x_{3} + \epsilon
$$

for prediction purposes:
$$
\hat{\gamma}= \beta_{0}+\beta_{1}x_{1}\beta_{2}x_{2}+\beta_{3}x_{3}
$$
where:
- $x_{1},x_{2},x_{3}$ represent the three [[Notes/explanatory variables\|predictors]].
- $\beta_{1},\beta_{2},\beta_{3}$ are the [[Notes/regression coefficient\|regression coefficients]]([[slopes\|slopes]]) associated with each [[Notes/explanatory variables\|independent variable]]
